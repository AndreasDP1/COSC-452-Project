# Paper index

This folder contains:
- source PDFs copied from the uploaded conversation files
- short working notes for each paper
- a quick "how we use this" mapping for the project

## Main uses
- Green (queueing + time-varying arrivals): motivate hourly arrival profiles and staffing-period logic
- Izady & Worthington (time-dependent queueing networks): motivate variable staffing over the day and warn that ED networks are more complex than single queues
- Ghanes et al. (DES + staffing optimization): motivate DES, budget, LOS, and an urgent-patient constraint
- Otto et al. (LOS as quality indicator): motivate time-in-system / LOS reporting
- ESI distribution paper: useful context for realistic triage-mix thinking, though not directly a staffing model
