# Izady & Worthington - time-dependent queueing networks

## Why we care
This paper is useful because it makes two important points:
- ED staffing is a time-dependent queueing problem
- EDs are networks, not just single queues

## Practical takeaway for our project
Even if our current baseline is one queue, the final writeup should explicitly say that this is a simplification.
This paper also supports the idea that staffing can vary by time block, which is exactly the direction we should use for a larger future genome.
