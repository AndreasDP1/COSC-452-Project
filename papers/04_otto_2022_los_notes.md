# Otto et al. - LOS as quality indicator

## Why we care
This paper is useful for justifying why time-in-system / LOS is worth reporting at all.

## Practical takeaway for our project
We should be careful with wording:
- if the simulator only covers triage, call the output "time in the triage subsystem"
- do not casually claim it is full ED LOS

The broader message is still useful:
- time-based process indicators matter
- congestion and long delays are quality concerns
