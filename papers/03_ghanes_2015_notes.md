# Ghanes et al. - simulation-based staffing optimization

## Why we care
This is probably the most directly relevant paper for the shape of our project.

It supports:
- discrete-event simulation
- staffing optimization
- budget-aware analysis
- using LOS/time-based metrics
- adding a second constraint so the objective is not perverse

## Practical takeaway for our project
Our report should probably say something like:
- weighted score is the baseline objective
- but urgent-service protection / scenario-wise fairness matters too

That is one reason we are keeping the door open for case-wise evaluation and not only one weighted sum.
